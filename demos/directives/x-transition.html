x-transition Directive

What is x-transition?

x-transition adds smooth CSS transitions when elements are shown or hidden. Works with x-show, x-if, and other visibility directives.

Example 1: Basic Fade Transition

Explanation: .duration modifier sets custom transition duration (e.g., .duration.500ms or .duration.2s).
